The Role
As a Software Engineer, you will gain hands-on experience working on real projects that span
the full software development lifecycle. You’ll collaborate with engineers, analysts,
developers, and industry experts, learning how to translate business needs into solutions that
improve usability, strengthen performance, and create tangible value for end users.
This role is designed to accelerate your technical growth and give you practical exposure to
modern engineering practices through high-impact, global technology projects.
What You’ll Do
• Contribute across the software development lifecycle: analysis, design, coding, testing,
deployment, and support.
• Write clean, efficient, and maintainable code aligned with best practices.
• Build and integrate features that enhance functionality and user experience.
• Support verification through unit and integration testing to ensure quality.
• Participate in debugging, troubleshooting, and live system improvements.
• Incorporate feedback from peers and stakeholders to strengthen solutions.
• Collaborate in agile teams, learning from mentorship and shared expertise.
• Assist in creating technical documentation and knowledge-sharing materials.
What We’re Looking For
• Pursuing B.E./B.Tech./M.E./M.Tech./M.Sc. in Computer Science, MCA, or other related
technical fields.
• Consistent academic performance (minimum 60% / 6 CGPA) with no active backlogs.
• Strong understanding of programming concepts, data structures, and algorithms.
• Familiarity with at least one programming language: Java, Python or C++.
• Analytical mindset, problem-solving skills, and eagerness to learn in a collaborative
environment.
Location:
Bengaluru, Chennai, Gurugram, Hyderabad, Mumbai, or any of our India collaboration centres.
